Classic Waffle

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of wheat flour

  • 2 tablespoon of sugar

  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

  • 2 spoons cornstarch

  • 3 beaten eggs

  • 4 tablespoons melted unsalted butter

  • 1 and 3/4 of east cup (tea)

  • 1 tablespoon vanilla essence

Preparation mode

  1. Sift the wheat flour, sugar, starch, yeast and salt into a container. Reserve.

  2. In another container, mix the beaten eggs with the egg, melted butter and vanilla essence.

  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly incorporate the ingredients.

  4. Heat the Waffle device Place a shallow ladle of dough and spread it until it covers the device mold,
    close the dish and bake until the dough is golden brown.

  5. Flatten with silicone spatulas Spread with honey, fruit or jam.

Additonal information

You can serve these waffles for breakfast with jam.
